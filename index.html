About Me

I’m a writer and photographer currently studying Economics at California State University, Long Beach. I’m not aiming to become an economist — my focus is creative. But I chose this degree because I believe understanding how systems and structures work gives my writing depth and relevance.

My academic background might lean analytical, but my work is rooted in intuition, observation, and storytelling. I document the quiet and chaotic moments of everyday life through both words and images.

This portfolio is a collection of recent essays, visual projects, and ongoing experiments. It’s where my creative side speaks freely — with clarity, edge, and intention.
